About Me

Hi, I'm Mia!

I’ve never really seen myself as much of a writer, which is kind of funny since I set up this blog. But here’s the deal—I love learning new things and figuring stuff out, and I wanted to share what I know in a straightforward way. So, I created this page to cut through the noise and give you the facts and tips without wasting hours hunting down the best solutions on the internet. Think of it as a little shortcut to getting stuff done.

I’m not one to follow a single path; I’ve always been drawn to adventure and discovery. I love travelling, meeting new people, and soaking up different cultures. There’s something amazing about stepping into the unknown and seeing the world from a fresh perspective.

One of my happy places? Definitely the mountains. Whether it’s hiking a tough trail, running through wild landscapes, or snapping photos of the view, the mountains are where I find peace and inspiration. There’s just something about reaching a peak that makes all the effort worth it.

On the academic side of things, I studied Electrical and Electronic Engineering. I’ve always been into computing and programming, but I wanted to understand the hardware side too—basically, how everything fits together. That curiosity led me to engineering, where I got to mix creativity, logic, and a bit of tech wizardry. If you’re curious, I’ve got some of my uni projects up on my GitHub, linked below.

When I’m not out exploring or working on a tech project, I’m probably daydreaming about my next adventure or planning a getaway to somewhere new.

If you ever want to chat about travel, tech, or finding the next big trail, feel free to reach out!
